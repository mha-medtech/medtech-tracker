The story behind MedTech Tracker

During my years as a medical devices inspector at the Iranian Food and Drug Organization, I visited hundreds of clinics. The same problem appeared everywhere: no one had a reliable system to track when equipment was last calibrated, when warranties were expiring, or which devices needed maintenance.

After founding Kayazh Medical, I saw this problem from the other side — as a distributor working with clinics daily. Most were using Excel sheets, paper notebooks, or nothing at all. Critical deadlines were missed. Equipment failed inspections. Warranties expired unnoticed.

MedTech Tracker is my answer to this problem. A simple, multilingual, mobile-friendly platform that any clinic can use — no technical expertise required.
